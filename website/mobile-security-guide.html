üîê Device Hardening Checklist

Essential Security Configurations:

  • ‚úì Enable device encryption (FileVault on iOS, Android encryption)
  • ‚úì Configure biometric authentication with PIN backup
  • ‚úì Disable USB debugging and developer options
  • ‚úì Enable automatic screen lock (max 5 minutes)
  • ‚úì Configure remote wipe capabilities
  • ‚úì Disable app installations from unknown sources
  • ‚úì Enable Find My Device/Find My iPhone
  • ‚úì Configure secure backup with encryption
  • ‚úì Disable lock screen notifications for sensitive apps
  • ‚úì Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts

üö® Incident Response Procedures

If Your Device is Compromised:

  1. Immediate Isolation: Disconnect from all networks
  2. Evidence Preservation: Don't delete anything
  3. Change Passwords: Update all account credentials
  4. Contact Support: Reach out to your IT team or security professional
  5. Document Everything: Record timeline and symptoms
  6. Professional Analysis: Have device forensically analyzed
